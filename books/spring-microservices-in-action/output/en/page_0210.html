If the targeted service name exists, it will return a weight and target destination of an alternative location for the service. The SpecialRoutesFilter will then take the weight returned and, based on the weight, randomly generate a number that will be used to determine whether the user’s call will be routed to the alternative organization service or to the organization service defined in the Zuul route mappings.
