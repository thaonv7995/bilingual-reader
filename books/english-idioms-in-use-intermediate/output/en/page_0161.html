55.4

These seem to be the basic meanings of these parts of the body in idioms – although some of the idioms have moved quite a long way from these concepts and indeed blood in particular may have several different sets of associations for the English speaker.

  • mind – thinking
  • blood – emotion (though is also used in idioms with the physical meaning of blood)
  • brain – intelligence
  • guts – intestines or your insides
