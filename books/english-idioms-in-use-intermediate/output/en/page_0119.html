56.1 Answer these questions.

  1. If your boss is always on your back about tidying your desk, is your desk usually tidy and how does your boss feel about this?
  2. If you put someone's back up, has your relationship with that person improved?
  3. What sort of thing might children do when the teacher's back is turned?
  4. If someone says You scratch my back and I'll scratch yours, what do they want you to do?
  5. If someone leaves home without a backward glance, are they happy to leave?
  6. If doing housework takes a back seat while you are revising for your exams, which is more important – housework or revision?
