48.2

Possible answers:

Check your answers with your teacher or another good speaker of English if you are not sure whether they are correct or not.

  1. I usually get itchy feet when the summer holidays start, and long to go away and travel.
  2. It took me a few weeks to find my feet in my new job.
  3. The dog is always under my feet when I'm getting ready for work.
  4. I landed on my feet when I met my friend Pat and he offered me a room in his house. It had been so difficult to find accommodation.
  5. I got off on the wrong foot with my teacher and we've never liked each other since the first day.
  6. I was rushed off my feet last week, but fortunately things are a bit quieter this week.
  7. I often get cold feet after I've made a big decision.
  8. I had to learn to stand on my own two feet when I left home and went to university.
  9. If I became very rich and famous, I hope I'd always be able to keep both feet on the ground.
