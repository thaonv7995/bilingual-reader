8.4

Possible answers:

1 This doesn't make sense - it would be annoying or upsetting to live next door to the neighbours from hell.
2 This doesn't make sense - being happy about something is the same as being cool with it.
3 This makes sense, as loving the painter's work is the opposite of it not floating the speaker's boat.
4 This doesn't make sense - nailing jelly to a wall means that something is hard to do; if someone is honest and open, then it is easy to see and understand their feelings and opinions.
