Other body idioms

I would give my right arm for a job in the film industry. I've always loved the movies. [would very much like to have]

I don't want to tread on your toes, but would it be OK if I added a few paragraphs to your report? [do something that could upset someone by getting involved in something that is their responsibility]

The media tend to point the finger at the government for most of the current problems. [accuse of being responsible for]

He gave an awful speech. He made lots of jokes but nobody laughed. It made my toes curl. [made you feel extremely embarrassed or ashamed for someone else]

You have to read the newspapers daily if you want to keep your finger on the pulse. [stay up-to-date]
