54.2

I’ve been offered a job that all my friends and family think I should take. The problem is that in my heart of hearts, I really don’t want it. It’s always been my heart’s desire to leave this city and work abroad, but I don’t have the heart to tell my family this, as I know they’ll be upset. I know you will tell me to take heart and be strong, but I really don’t want to hurt them. After all, I know they have my best interests at heart. What should I do?
