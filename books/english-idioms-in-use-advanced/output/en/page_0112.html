52.2

Read these statements and answer the questions.

1
Richard said it was no picnic for someone of his age to get used to a new job.
Was it easy or difficult for Richard when he started his new job?
2
Lena is very nice, but she's no oil painting.
Is Lena pretty?
3
John is very good at squash, even though he's no spring chicken.
Is John still a young man?
4
People often laugh at Georgie, but she's nobody's fool.
How easy would it be to deceive Georgie?
5
Luke's boss told him in no uncertain terms that he'd be dismissed immediately if he didn't work hard enough.
How direct was Luke's boss with his threats?
6
It's no joke trying to bring up a family on the minimum wage.
How easy is it to look after a family if your only income is the minimum wage?
