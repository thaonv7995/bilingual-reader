I’m training to be a vet, and I’ve got ten exams, all told, to prepare for. It means I’ve been studying at all hours of the day and night. I’m exhausted, and to cap it all I’ve got three exams on the same day this week! I just want to relax and go on holiday, but all in good time – I’ll be finished next month.

I told my friend I was feeling stressed, but he just laughed – that’s him all over; he never takes anything seriously, and even when he’s worried or anxious he’s all smiles.
