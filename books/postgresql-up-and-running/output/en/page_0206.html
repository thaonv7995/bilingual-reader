Querying Nonconventional Data Sources

The database world does not appear to be getting more homogeneous. Exotic databases are sprouting up faster than we can keep tabs on. Some are fads and quickly drown in their own hype. Some aspire to dethrone relational databases altogether. Some could hardly be considered databases. The introduction of FDWs is in part a response to the growing diversity. FDW assimilates without compromising the PosgreSQL core.
