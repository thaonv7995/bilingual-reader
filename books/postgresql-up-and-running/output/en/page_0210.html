FreeBSD

FreeBSD is a popular platform for PostgreSQL. However, many people who use FreeBSD tend to compile their own directly from source rather than using a packaged distribu­tion. You can find the latest beta versions of PostgreSQL at FreeBSD.

Mac OS X

We’ve seen a variety of ways to install PostgreSQL on Macs. EnterpriseDB offers an installer. The Homebrew is gaining popularity and attracts advanced Mac users. Kyng­Chaos is suitable for folks looking for an up-to-date and complete open source GIS experience. Postgres.app is a newcomer geared for the novice. The long-standing Mac­Ports and Fink distributions are still around. We do advise against mixing installers for Mac users.
