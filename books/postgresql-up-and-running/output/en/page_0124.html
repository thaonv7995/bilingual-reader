Although we didn’t demonstrate it here, you can overload functions and operators to take different types as inputs. For example, you can create an add function and com‐ panion + operator that takes a complex_number and an integer.

The ability to build custom types and operators pushes PostgreSQL to the boundary of a full-fledged development environment, bringing us ever closer to our utopia where everything is table-driven.
