Querying Other PostgreSQL Servers

The PostgreSQL FDW, postgres_fdw, is packaged with most distributions of Post­greSQL 9.3. This FDW allows you to read as well as push updates to other PostgreSQL servers, even different versions.

Anyone who can connect to your database will be able to access the foreign server as well. The role you map to must exist on the foreign server and have login rights.
