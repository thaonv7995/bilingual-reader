However, even a time written in this format can be inconvenient when people are spread out geographically and are in different time zones. For example, if you are in New York, you may be befuddled if you are asked to dial in for a team meeting at 2PM UTC on 29th March 2024! You would expect to see the time in your time zone, i.e., 10AM EST. Of course, your remote colleague in India may not be too happy to see that they have to dial in at 7.30PM but that’s a different issue!
