16.1.6 Collection and List API☝

Collection API☝

java.util.Collection is the root interface in the collections hierarchy. It abstracts the concept of a collection in general. It declares only the methods that are applicable to all sorts of collections and leaves the methods that deal with specialized features such as ordering or uniqueness of elements to sub-interfaces such as List and Set .
