Translating between ZonedDateTime and OffsetDateTime

Since ZonedDateTime and OffsetDateTime both contain a zone id (remember that ZoneOffset extends ZoneId), it is possible to translate either of these two types to the other. However, recall that the ZoneId of an OffsetDateTime does not really correspond to an actual region. It just contains the offset amount (such as “-4:00”).
